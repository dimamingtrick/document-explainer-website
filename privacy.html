Privacy policy

1. Introduction

Welcome to Document Explainer! Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our application.

2. Information We Collect

  • Files and Content: We collect files, images, photos, and text that you upload to the application. These are stored until you delete them or your account.
  • Account Information: We collect your email address, username, and other details necessary for account creation and management.
  • Usage Data: We collect anonymized usage data to improve application performance and user experience.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To analyze uploaded files using AI technology.
  • To personalize your experience and provide relevant features.
  • To display advertisements within the application.
  • To improve the AI model through anonymized data analysis.
  • To manage subscriptions and provide premium features.

4. Data Storage and Retention

  • Your files are stored securely and retained until you delete your account or request removal.
  • Account information is retained as long as your account is active or as required by law.

5. Sharing of Information

  • We do not share your files, images, or content with third parties unless required by law or with your explicit consent.
  • Aggregated and anonymized usage data may be shared for analytics and performance optimization.

6. Advertising

  • We display advertisements within the application.
  • Ads may be personalized based on your usage patterns.
  • You can remove ads by subscribing to a premium plan.

7. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

  • We use cookies and similar technologies to enhance your experience.
  • Cookies help remember your preferences and improve application performance.
  • You can manage cookie settings through your device preferences.

8. Security Measures

  • We implement industry-standard security practices to protect your data.
  • Despite our best efforts, no system is completely secure. We encourage users to use strong passwords and remain vigilant.

9. Your Rights

  • You can access, edit, or delete your files at any time.
  • You can request account deletion, which will result in the permanent removal of all your data within 30 days.
  • You can opt out of personalized advertising through your account settings.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be communicated through the application. Continued use after updates indicates your acceptance of the changes.

11. Contact Us
